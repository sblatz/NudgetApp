Finally. An expense tracker for the rest of us.

Jotting down the price of your coffee shouldn't require so many taps. Nudget puts a focus on speed and simplicity so you can quickly record a purchase and never skip a beat.

Nudget is an expense tracker with a minimalist design that puts you in control of your finances, once and for all. With quick entry for purchases, recording expenses never feels like a chore, so you'll actually stay in the habit. And as you continue to use the app, Nudget will coach you on how to better reach financial health.

Powerful insights without the spreadsheets.

Nudget analyzes trends in your spending and brings them front and center in the Insights tab. Insights take all the guess work out of your spending habits, empowering you to make smarter decisions about your financial goals.

The features you need.
And then some.

Cents or Dollars

You decide how to record expenses. For quick entry, you can record just dollar amounts.

iCloud Sync

With iCloud sync, your data is always secure and available, even if your phone takes a plunge.

Fixed Expenses

Recurring expenses are a huge pain to keep track of. That's why Nudget helps you maintain a list of them within the app.

Dance in the Dark

With a gorgeous selection of themes to choose from, you can customize exactly how you'd like Nudget to look.

Any Currency

No matter which country you reside in, Nudget has support for your currency.

Export Data

Itching to play around with your data outside of Nudget? Just export your expenses in the settings menu.
